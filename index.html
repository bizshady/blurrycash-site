About

Whats the story here?

Blurrycash was created by forking the Nerva (XNV) Fork Blur's chain at block 266267. This means that Blur private keys or seeds are compatible with our network and once restored you will have coins on both chains. We believe that the project should go in a different direction before it can no longer be fixed thus damaging the reputation of its parents in the process.

Read more about why below.

Securing a blockchain

Part of the community believes that recent events show that development of Blur is misguided. This is an attempt to right the course before it and its parent are damaged. Blurrycash will adopt the Ticker BLCH while keeping the original address prefix.

Community Led

We want this project to be led by the community for the community. True decentralization while working closely with its parent to improve the whole cryptocurrency space.
