About

Whats the story here?

Blurrycash was created by forking the Nerva (XNV) Fork Blur's chain at block 266267. This means that the keys of holders of Blur will work on our network. We believe the project should go in another direction before it and its parent are tarnished beyond repair.

Read more about why below.

Securing a blockchain

Part of the community believes that recent events show that development of Blur is misguided. This is an attempt to right the course before it and its parent are damaged. Blurrycash will adopt the Ticker BLCH while keeping the original address prefix.

Community Led

We want this project to be led by the community for the community. True decentralization while working closely with its parent to improve the whole cryptocurrency space as a whole.

Close Collaboration
